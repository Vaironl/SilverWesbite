About Us

Crime Hot Spot is being developed by Old Dominion University's 2018-2019 Team Silver in an effort to make crime mapping more user-friendly and accessible to the average person.

Team Silver consists of nine ODU students enrolled in Professor Thomas Kennedy's Professional Workforce Development course sequence. We believe that information regarding crime and safety should not only be available, it should also be understandable. Most publicly available crime mapping programs do not offer this important information in a concise, user-friendly manner. In response to this, our team has embarked on a journey to create a better crime mapping experience through redesigned interfaces, displays, and user options.

Key features of our project include


A heatmap display of crime to replace the clutter of icon-based displays. See Feasibility Presentation.
A SafetyScore algorithm to determine the threat a crime represents. See Design Handout.
A user interface designed for both fast basic usage and in-depth customization. See GUI Demo.

If you want to learn more about our project, please visit our Presentations page. We look forward to hearing your feedback and wish you a low crime experience.
