Crime Hot Spot

Learn how safe an area really is!

Problem Statement

Current crime maps do not display data in a way that allows the user to efficiently and effectively make informed decisions about their safety.

Solution

An app that shows the crimes in an area using a heat map instead of icons. The color of a region will signify the intensity of the crimes committed in that location. Blue can represent less crimes and red more crimes. This intensity is called the crime safety score and each point on the map will have its own unique crime safety score. The scores value can be calculated in terms of: the severity of the surrounding crimes the type of each crime committed the distance of the crimes from that point the length of time since each crime occured and the time of day and day of the week that each crime was committed in relation to now.
